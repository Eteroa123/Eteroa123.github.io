What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

Margin is know as the space that is outside something. So picture a drawn box and picture three boxes inside this box. Margin is the outer box. When adding in margin you are given access to make the margin wider and longer.

The border goes around the padding and element box. If we can picture again the drawn box then we can picture the border inside the margin box. Borders are important not just because its to make it look pretty. But you can change the spacing and also the width of the border.

The padding goes inside the margin and border areas, and outside the content area. So that is what and where the padding is. And with padding you are able to remove space. For example I added table and I wanted to move it up. By playing around with the Padding I was able to make it look tight and close.
